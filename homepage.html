About Us

Discover our story in providing camping services.

Our Story

Our journey began with a shared passion for enhancing disaster response and saving lives. We witnessed the need for a unified platform to connect rescue agencies during crises, and this drove us to develop our application.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize disaster relief by providing a single, reliable platform where all rescue agencies can register and collaborate seamlessly. We aim to expedite response efforts, minimize loss, and ensure the safety of affected communities during calamities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where our application is the go-to resource for disaster response coordination, where rescue agencies work together effortlessly to mitigate the impact of natural and man-made disasters, ultimately saving lives and reducing suffering.
